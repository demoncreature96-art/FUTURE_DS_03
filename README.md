# Marketing Funnel & Lead Conversion Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the marketing funnel of Olist leads to identify:

- Funnel drop-off points
- Lead conversion performance
- High-quality acquisition channels
- Lead quality by segment and behavior
- Monthly conversion trends

The dashboard was developed using Power BI and Python.

---

## Business Questions

- Where are users dropping off in the funnel?
- Which channels bring high-quality leads?
- Which lead types convert best?
- Which business segments generate the most conversions?
- How can conversion rates be improved?

---

## Dataset

Source: Olist Marketing Funnel Dataset

Main tables analyzed:

- Marketing Qualified Leads (MQLs)
- Closed Deals
- Lead Attributes

---

- Power BI

---

## Key Findings

### Funnel Performance

- Total Leads: 8,000
- Converted Leads: 842
- Conversion Rate: 10.53%
- Drop-Off Rate: 89.48%

### Channel Performance

Top Converting Channels:

1. Paid Search (12.30%)
2. Organic Search (11.80%)
3. Direct Traffic (11.22%)

### Lead Quality

- online_medium generated the highest number of won deals.
- Resellers accounted for approximately 70% of all won deals.
- home_decor was the highest-performing business segment.

### Trend Analysis

- Conversion rate improved from below 1% to approximately 14.5%.
- Lead volume increased significantly in 2018.
- Strong evidence of improving sales efficiency over time.

## Business Recommendations

- Increase investment in Paid Search and Organic Search.
- Improve lead nurturing to reduce the 89.48% funnel drop-off.
- Focus sales efforts on high-performing business segments.
- Investigate factors behind the conversion rate increase in early 2018.
- Optimize low-performing channels such as Email and Social.
